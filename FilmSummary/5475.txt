But Mr. Filmore\'s unscrupulous chauffeur, Lester Lewis, takes advantage of Phillip\'s crush on Nicole to hire her to seduce the youth, then draws her into a plot to fake her own death in a blackmail scheme aimed to drain Phillip\'s trust fund.
