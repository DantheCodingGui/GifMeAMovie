andes to amazon is an exploration of the exciting diverse and unique wildlife that inhabits the dramatic landscapes of the vast south american continent its a land of great extremes stretching from the antarctic to the equator it has the planets greatest river system longest mountain chain biggest and richest rainforest and driest desert using the latest camera techniques including infrared nightvision cameras rarely seen animals are revealed whilst a specialist aerial cameraman soars over the continent revealing an entirely new perspective on its varied and dramatic landscape
