this weeks program is about an exceptional woman who grew up just down the road from the victorian high country her father would have preferred a son and he raised leigh woodgate to be tougher than all her male contemporaries leigh woodgate became a renowned and fearless horsewoman who excelled at bush racing she soon graduated to the hazardous sport of steeplechasing or jumps racing and became something of a celebrity but when disaster struck she stunned the racing establishment with courage and determination of quite epic proportions unfolding over two decades
