lawstinwoods follows the story of 6 strangers who were taken from their lives and placed in woods called the lawstin woods the mysterious name of the woods happens to parallel their own misfortune which is that they are all lost in woods while trapped in the mystical forest they struggle to get answers while they fight to stay alive all the while a small group of people who claim to be residents of the lawstin woods taunt those whore lost but never give the gang answers instead they appear to simply toy with them
