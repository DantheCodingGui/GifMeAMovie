follows the adventures of elevenyearold zach nichols and tenyearold annie redfeather as they suffer pain from issues involving other children to solve these problems they seek council from a group of anthropomorphic animals whose names come from the greek  plato an old and scholarly bison aurora a wise redtailed hawk socrates a rambunctious and wisecracking bobcat and aristotle a feisty and bookish prairie dog this ensemble teaches the kids virtues by telling them stories from the book of virtues
