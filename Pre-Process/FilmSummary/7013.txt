"\nFour would-be siblings are encouraged by their parents to bond during an outdoor vacation. The four adventurers journey into the wild on their ATVs in search of a family relic. They soon find themselves lost in the woods and ill-equipped to deal with not only the rugged terrain but each other. This is a story of hope and overcoming, full of humor and fun. It's a coming of age tale not only for kids but for parents as well.                ",
