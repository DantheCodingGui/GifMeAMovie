in the second of a threepart series uncovering the minds behind some of the greatest scientific discoveries of our age james lovelock explains how his maverick way of thinking led him not only to technical breakthroughs in atmospheric detection systems on earth and mars but also to gaia  a new way of thinking about the earth as a holistic selfregulating system he tells of his struggle against the scientific consensus of the day the ridicule of his peers and his passionate belief that the mainstream scientific establishment stifles intellectual creativity
