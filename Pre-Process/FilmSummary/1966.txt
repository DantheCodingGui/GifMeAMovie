500 nations is an eight part documentary which explores the history of the indigenous peoples of north and central america from precolombian times through the period of european contact and colonization to the end of the 19th century and the subjugation of the plains indians of north america 500 nations relies on historical texts eyewitnesses accounts pictorial sources and computer graphic reconstructions to explore the magnificent civilizations which flourished prior to contact with western civilization and to tell the dramatic and tragic story of the native american nations desperate attempts to retain their way of life against overwhelming odds
