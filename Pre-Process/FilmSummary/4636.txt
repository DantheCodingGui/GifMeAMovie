a woman of substance charts the life of emma harte from kitchen maid at the beginning of the 20th century to respected business woman and grandmother in the 1980s from humble beginnings emma harte starts her business with a small shop but over the next twenty years she expands her stores and invests in the growing textile industry in leeds by the time of world war 2 emma is the head of a major retail and manufacturing empire but she has struggled all her life to find love after an illegitimate daughter and two marriages she finally meets the love of her life paul mcgill but their affair is cut short by a tragic accident leaving emma with his daughter in the 1980s emma faces one of her biggest tests  her childrens attempt to remove her as head of her company but emma is far from the senile old woman they think she is  she is determined to stop them at all costs
