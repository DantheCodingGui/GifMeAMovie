"\nBased on the masterpiece, American short story, Walking Out, David (Josh Wiggins), a typical teenage urbanite travels to rural Montana to go hunting with his estranged, 'off the grid' father, Cal (Matt Bomer). As they ascend deep into the wilderness, father and son struggle to connect on any level. When an unexpected encounter with grizzly bears leaves them both with serious injuries, the boy must carry his father to safety if they are to survive.            "
