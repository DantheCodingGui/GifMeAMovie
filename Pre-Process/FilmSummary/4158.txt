in connecticut arne johnson and debbie glatzel are a young couple eager to start their new life together in a rental house they bring debbies young brother david over to help them clean it but after spending a brief time in the bedroom sweeping the boy runs out of the house in fear he claims there is an old man in the bedroom later when they are all back at the home of debbies mother where debbie and arne are living temporarily david claims that the old man has followed him there no one believes him but soon paranormal occurrences in the house convince them that david is telling the truth it becomes clear that the old man is really a demon at first he physically attacks david then he takes possession of the boy the family calls in the paranormal researchers ed and lorraine warren to investigate they in turn call in a priest to perform an exorcism
