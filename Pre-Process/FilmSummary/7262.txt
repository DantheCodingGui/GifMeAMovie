alaska filmmakers is a program dedicated to highlighting the various talented individuals in alaska film industry a series for filmmakers by filmmakers about filmmakers our hosts sit down with local independent filmmakers and productions groups to talk about what inspired them to become involved in the production industry exploring the various challenges and triumphs of alaskas storytellers and how they are working together to help sustain the states film industry created by a small group of local artists who have all worked with one another at some point in their careers pulling all their resources each member of the crew has brought their own experiences and unique style to the program
