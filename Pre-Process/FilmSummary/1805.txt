\nForty-two year old famed composer/playwright George Webber is going through a midlife crisis. He is seriously dating thirty-eight year old actress/singer Samantha Taylor, who he loves, although he admits their connection is more intellectual than it is emotional. She, in turn, loves him, despite barely tolerating his often infantile behavior. This behavior includes spying on a neighbor\'s sexual encounters with a wide array of women, this spying about which the neighbor knows, as he does it himself. Driving one day, George spots a young woman who he believes is the most beautiful creature he\'s ever seen - an "eleven" on a scale of ten, tens which he didn\'t believe existed before her. Beyond the fact that she is probably half his age, a problem with George\'s infatuation is that she is just off to her own wedding.
