in the cutthroat world of pro soccer a club lives and dies by the stars on its under21 team they are the future and lifeblood of any franchise but most will never make it 21 thunder is the story of the montreal thunder u21 team following the teams star players on and off the field a story of love crime race sex and athletic glory at its core the series is about how a group of players and coaches unite as family in the whirlwind of life one step away from the pros
