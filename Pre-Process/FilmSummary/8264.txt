"\nAfter losing his family Jack Coleman settles into addiction and his life quickly spirals out of control. When Jack's brother, a colonel in the Air Force, appears unexpectedly tasking Jack with keeping his wife and daughter alive during the impending apocalypse, Jack's forced to reckon with his addiction. I Am Jack's Apocalypse is a journey into the mind and soul of a man who's lost everything as he struggles to stay alive.                ",
