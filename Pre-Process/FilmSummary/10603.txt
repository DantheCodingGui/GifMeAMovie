a look at the life the work and the contributions of gene kelly to movies and movie making the film celebrates kellys putting storytelling into dance and discovering along with martha graham and jerome robbins an american style it examines his partnership with stanley dolan his bringing of tap and ballet into musicals his marriages and personal competitiveness his political views and his work on stage as well as in film this production details his contributions as a dancer choreographer and director the narration and talking heads also discuss his muscular style his low center of gravity and his masculine appeal
