in this black comedy with a twist elderly retired cop art bellasco and retired private eye jimmie thurson cant stand each other and spend their days in the sunset acres retirementnursing home playing gin and arguing with each other however the only thing they cant stand even more is the sunset acres home itself and its director dr stern whos in it for the money the only people who seem to care about them are nurse maxwell and their silent friend boots one night art and jimmie make a pact whoever dies first gets the other persons lifesavings even if one kills the other and gets to finally leave the accursed place and travel to some dreamy and fancy beach resort in france this sets off a deadly competition between them meanwhile dr stern tries to land a new client  uptight elderly book lover mrs haverford but arts and jimmies duel keeps getting in the way
