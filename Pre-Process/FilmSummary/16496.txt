born to style is a lifestyle makeover series centered on a fierce team from new york and their larger than life boss jonathan bodrick springing from the harlembased eclectic boutique born borrowed old refurbished and new the style superheroes land at the door of those desperately in need of some color in their lives by rifling through their clients belongings and asking blunt questions the team at born gets to the root of their fashion malaise before whisking them away for a transformation that goes beyond just their look in each episode viewers will see them offer up their own brand of therapy and inspiration to clients as they transform them into their best possible selves
