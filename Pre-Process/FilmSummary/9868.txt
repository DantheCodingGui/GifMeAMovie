in american caravan six young americans journey across the middle east in the footsteps of revolution the narrative is driven by the casts quest to meet and be inspired by the arab youth who started it all but almost immediately the casts fearlessness is at odds with the producers conservative safe plans for them and what starts out as an exercise in public diplomacy and reality television quickly turns in to a headlong collision with the arab spring and a oneofakind story of american and arab youth as they travel to tunisia egypt lebanon oman and the united arab emirates for everyone it becomes a life changing experience with high drama danger adventure conflict joy sadness music and love
