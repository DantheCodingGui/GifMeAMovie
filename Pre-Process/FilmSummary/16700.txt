infected mushroom is an israeli psychedelic trance duo formed by erez aizen also known as izen and amit duvdevani also known as duvdev in the city of haifa located in north israel the duo has garnered a large international fan base infected mushroom are known for their consistent sonic evolution exemplified by the subsequent albums the gathering 1999 classical mushroom 2000 bp empire 2001 converting vegetarians 2003 im the supervisor 2004 and vicious delicious 2007
