amazing medical stories follows the lives of people with rare and surprising medical afflictions and documents the remarkable medical treatments that are used to cure them from a man falling into a churning cement mixer to a baby born with four legs each episode tells stories that range from unusual accidents to oneofakind conditions each story begins as a medical mystery gradually revealing the condition and symptoms until finally the right physician comes along to make a diagnostic breakthrough this series is an inspiring celebration of science and human inventiveness triumphing over illness accidents and disease
