ricky jay is a worldrenowned magician author historian and actor often a mischievous presence in the films of david mamet and paul thomas anderson  and a performer who regularly provokes astonishment from even the most jaded audiences deceptive practice traces jays achievements and influences from his apprenticeship at age 4 with his grandfather to such nowforgotten legends as al flosso slydini cardini and his primary mentors dai vernon and charlie miller featuring rare footage from his 1970s tv appearances doing 3card monte with steve martin on the dinah shore show and told in jays inimitable voice this is a remarkable journey inside the secretive world of magic and the small circle of eccentrics who are its perpetual devotees
