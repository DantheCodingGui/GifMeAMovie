the chosen one is a story of love loss and high adventure in the world of australias fast jets the story centres on a young man jeremy mcness who fulfills his boyhood dream of becoming one of the air forces elite f111 pilots but when jeremy is killed along with his navigator in a night time exercise his mother jan sets out on a decade long mission to uncover the truth about the circumstances of the crash in the process she becomes an unlikely expert on f111s and air force procedures
