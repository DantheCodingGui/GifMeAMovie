2613 is a suspense thriller and action show that revolves around the planning of a terror plot shahana a strongwilled woman whose aim is to make the 2613 mission a success randeep is the special task force agent who aids rashmi in her endeavour to thwart the 2613 plot will shahana manage to execute the terror plot or will rashmi succeed in preventing it this is the story of rashmi a common girl turned special agent fights back terrorism
