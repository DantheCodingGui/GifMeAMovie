before barinia transits the corinth canal patrick and narelle will take you to meet pagona and her son tassos who run a family taverna and make possibly the best seafood pasta in the entire mediterranean  according to the skipper the skipper will then motor barinia through the corinth canal and into the gulf of corinth where they will be literally escorted into delphi by delphini dolphins here patrick and narelle will take you to a yachtie hideaway for a magnificent breakfast with chrissoula at the hotel ganimede after an ouzo and a game of backgammon they will sail into the night and as a tribute to greece narelle will prepare a crew favorite in the galley  moussaka barinia will then arrive in argostoli  the last greek port where the crew will prepare the yacht for the long crossing of the ionian sea to italy
