around the next bend is the story of two friends embarking on a remarkable personal journey far from home adrian traquair and his good friend dustin corkery are two regular guys from the small city of regina saskatchewan canada driven by a sense of adventure and excitement they travel halfway around the world to embark on a lifechanging journey from the waterway of one of the worlds largest and holiest rivers they begin to discover the magnificent country of india and the unique people that inhabit it they will grow to gain a greater understanding of themselves and the world they live in as they slowly raft down the mystic ganges river
